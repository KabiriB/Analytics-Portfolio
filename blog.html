Welcome to My Blog

Organisations today are drowning in data. From customer analytics and real-time dashboards to AI-driven predictions, businesses and policymakers alike have placed data at the centre of their decision-making frameworks. But does more data always lead to better decisions?

This blog series explores the promises and pitfalls of data-driven decision-making, particularly in business strategy and societal development. Drawing from leading research and case studies, I examine how data can drive competitive advantage, improve decision-making, and transform industries—but also where it falls short, reinforcing biases and misleading decision-makers.

Key Takeaways from the Blog Series

  • Data Alone Is Not a Competitive Advantage – Simply having data isn’t enough; it’s how effectively it’s used that matters.
  • The Pitfalls of Data Over-Reliance – AI-generated insights can amplify biases rather than eliminate them.
  • Smart Data Usage in Emerging Markets – Mobile tracking and alternative data sources are reshaping decision-making and economic development.
