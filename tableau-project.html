Executive Retail Sales Analysis

Overview

This Executive Retail Sales Analysis reveals a business with strong revenue performance ($1.032M YTD sales) but profitability challenges due to inefficient discounting and regional variations. While the West and East regions drive the highest sales, some cities and product categories struggle with low margins or losses, indicating opportunities for pricing optimisation and strategic product bundling. Sales follow a seasonal pattern, peaking in December, yet profitability varies widely month-to-month, suggesting operational inefficiencies during peak demand periods. To drive sustainable growth, the company should shift from volume-driven sales to profit-focused strategies, refining discounting, inventory, and regional marketing efforts while capitalising on high-margin products and strategic pricing adjustments.
