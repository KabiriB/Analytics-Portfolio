Power BI Project: Supermarket Sales Analysis

Overview

This Power BI project provides a data-driven analysis of supermarket sales , helping uncover key revenue trends, customer behavior, and performance insights across different locations. The report explores city-wise sales distribution, revenue trends over time, top product performance, and payment preferences to guide business decisions.

Key Insights

  • Sales Performance by City:
    Among the three branches, Naypyitaw leads in total revenue with 111K in sales , followed closely by Yangon and Mandalay, both at 106K . This suggests a balanced sales distribution but highlights potential opportunities for further optimization.
  • Revenue Trends Over Time:
    The sales timeline shows consistent fluctuations , indicating seasonal demand shifts. Notable peaks in revenue correspond to specific sales periods , promotional campaigns, or external factors such as holidays. Identifying these trends can help in predicting future sales spikes and optimizing inventory .
  • Top-Performing Product Lines:
    The most revenue-generating product categories include Food & Beverage, Sports & Travel, and Electronic Accessories . These products consistently drive sales, suggesting opportunities to introduce bundling, targeted promotions, or loyalty rewards for repeat purchases.
  • Customer Payment Preferences:
    Sales data highlights a diverse range of payment methods , with cash and e-wallets being the most frequently used. Credit card payments account for a smaller share , presenting an opportunity to introduce incentives for card transactions to boost adoption.
  • Customer Segmentation & Purchase Behavior:
    Analysis of customer type shows that members tend to spend more across all payment methods, reinforcing the value of loyalty programs . Expanding membership benefits could further drive customer retention and repeat purchases .
  • Sales Trends by Time:
    Daily and monthly sales trends reveal fluctuations , with specific periods seeing sharp revenue spikes. These patterns emphasize the importance of seasonal promotions, stock optimization, and demand forecasting .

Strategic Recommendations

  • Expand Targeted Marketing in Underperforming Cities:
    While Naypyitaw leads in revenue, Yangon and Mandalay show potential for growth . Implementing localized promotions, customer engagement campaigns, and community outreach can drive higher sales in these areas.
  • Optimize Inventory & Supply Chain Management:
    Given the revenue peaks and fluctuations , ensuring optimal stock levels is crucial. Leveraging predictive analytics to anticipate high-demand periods can prevent stockouts and overstocking issues , enhancing supply chain efficiency.
  • Introduce Payment-Based Promotions:
    With cash and e-wallets dominating transactions, introducing discounts or cashback incentives for credit card payments could encourage diversification and improve checkout flexibility.
  • Enhance Customer Loyalty Programs:
    Since members show higher spending patterns , expanding membership benefits, such as exclusive discounts, personalized offers, and reward points , can boost repeat purchases and improve customer retention.
  • Leverage Sales Timing for Maximum Impact:
    Aligning promotional activities with historically high-sales periods can maximize revenue . Implementing flash sales, limited-time offers, and holiday discounts at peak demand times will enhance customer engagement.

Power BI Project: Business Revenue Analysis

Overview

Analysis of AdventureWorks' sales data to identify revenue trends, profitability, and product performance.

Key Insights

  • Revenue Performance: Total revenue stands at $24.9M, with $10.5M in profit and a 2.2% return rate.
  • Order Trends: 25.2K orders have been placed, with monthly revenue at $1.83M, showing a 3.31% increase from the previous month.
  • Product Category Performance: Accessories lead with 17K orders, followed by Bikes (13.9K orders) and Clothing (7K orders).
  • The Water Bottle (30 oz.) is the most sold product (3,983 orders), and Tires and Tubes are the most frequently ordered product type.
  • Return Trends: The highest return rate (3.33%) is seen in Sport-100 Helmet (Red and Blue); Shorts are the most returned product type.

Strategic Recommendations

Optimize Inventory Management

  • Increase stock of Tires and Tubes due to high demand.
  • Review and adjust stock levels for products with high return rates, such as helmets and shorts.

Enhance Sales Strategy

  • Capitalize on revenue growth by expanding sales strategies for high-performing categories (Accessories and Bikes).
  • Monitor seasonal trends in revenue fluctuations to optimize pricing and promotions.

Reduce Return Rates

  • Investigate reasons for high returns on helmets and shorts (e.g., sizing issues, quality concerns).
  • Improve product descriptions and customer guidance to minimize returns.
