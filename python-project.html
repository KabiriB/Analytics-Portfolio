Python Project: HDI & GDP Analysis

Overview

Exploring the relationship between human development and economic growth.

Key Insights

  • GDP is positively correlated with HDI, but governance and policies shape outcomes.
  • Sub-Saharan Africa has a cluster of low GDP & HDI countries, whereas EAP & ECA show more variation.
  • Countries like Bhutan and Cabo Verde excel in HDI despite modest GDP, showing effective social investment.

Strategic Recommendations

  • Prioritize investments in education, healthcare, and welfare for higher HDI.
  • Use data-driven governance to close development gaps.
  • Analyze high-HDI, low-GDP nations for best practices.
